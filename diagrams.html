Velocity Addition

Special relativity introduces plenty of complexity, but spacetime diagrams, an event-based approach, and Lorentz transformations are helpful tools for making sense of various scenarios. Furthermore, we can use events and diagrams to show that at small speeds, relativity's equations for length, duration, and velocity addition reduce naturally to the more familiar classical mechanics equations.
